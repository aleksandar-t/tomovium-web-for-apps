BoredOnPlane Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 21, 2026

Overview

BoredOnPlane is an offline-first airplane activity app by Tomovium. Most app content, saved trip packs, game activity, and local progress stay on your device. Online services are used only for app configuration, optional AI trip-pack generation, Google Play purchase verification, entitlement and credit checks, abuse prevention, diagnostics, and support.

Data We Process

When the app connects to Tomovium Core, we may process a generated installation identifier, app code, app version, platform, package name, request timestamps, entitlement status, credit balances, usage counters, and technical diagnostics such as failed request reasons. The installation identifier is generated for app operation and is not your Google account, email address, phone number, or advertising identifier.

For Google Play purchases, the app sends the product id, package name, and Google Play purchase token to Tomovium Core. We verify the purchase server-side with Google Play and store purchase records needed to grant, restore, revoke, refund, troubleshoot, and support access.

Server logs and security events may include technical request metadata such as timestamps, route names, app identifiers, rate-limit decisions, and network information used to protect the service and investigate abuse or support issues.

AI Trip Packs

If you choose to generate an AI trip pack, the app sends the trip details needed for generation, such as origin, destination, departure date, flight length, travel mood, locale, and a local trip identifier. This data is used to generate offline activities for that trip.

AI requests are processed by Tomovium Core and may be sent to an AI provider such as Google Gemini. AI providers process the submitted prompt and generated response so the feature can work. Do not enter sensitive personal, medical, legal, financial, or confidential information into AI trip-pack requests.

Local And Offline Data

Generated packs, built-in activities, local game progress, and trip preparation content may be stored on your device so the app works in airplane mode. Deleting the app or clearing its storage may remove local content. Tomovium may not be able to recover content that exists only on your device.

How We Use Data

We use data to operate the app, verify purchases, restore access, manage Pro entitlements and trip-pack credits, generate optional AI content, enforce limits, prevent abuse, diagnose problems, respond to support requests, and keep the service secure and sustainable.

Sharing And Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. We share data only as needed to operate the app and its services. Google Play is used for purchases and purchase verification. Google Gemini or another configured AI provider may be used for optional AI generation. Hosting, database, logging, and infrastructure services may be provided by cloud platforms used by Tomovium Core.

Security And Abuse Prevention

Data sent between the app and Tomovium Core is transmitted over HTTPS. Tomovium Core uses server-side purchase verification, anonymous installation tokens, rate limits, request limits, entitlement checks, usage tracking, security events, and access controls to protect the app, prevent abuse, and control infrastructure cost.

Retention And Deletion

Local app data remains on your device until you delete it, clear app storage, or uninstall the app. Server-side records such as installation identifiers, purchase records, entitlement records, credit ledger entries, usage counters, diagnostics, and security events may be retained as long as needed to operate the service, restore purchases, prevent fraud, comply with platform requirements, resolve disputes, and maintain accurate audit history.

You can contact us to request deletion of server-side records associated with your app installation where deletion is technically possible and not required for security, fraud prevention, purchase support, accounting, legal compliance, or audit reasons.

What We Do Not Do

We do not require an account for the current app experience. We do not use your Google Play purchase token to identify your Google account email. We do not provide AI provider API keys to the mobile app. We do not sell personal data.

Children

BoredOnPlane is not intended to collect personal information from children. If a child uses the app, a parent or guardian should supervise what is entered into optional AI features.

Changes

We may update this policy when the app, backend, providers, or legal requirements change. The updated date above shows when this policy was last changed.

Contact

For privacy questions, purchase support, app feedback, or deletion requests related to server-side records, contact support@tomovium.com.
