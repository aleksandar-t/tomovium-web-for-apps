Privacy Policy Support

Built For Airplane Mode

Core activities are designed to work locally on your device. The app should still be useful when the cabin door closes and the network disappears.

Optional AI Trip Packs

When enabled, the app can ask Tomovium Core to generate a personalized offline pack for a trip, then save that pack locally for later use.

Simple Purchases

Google Play purchases can unlock Pro access or add trip-pack credits. Purchase verification happens through Tomovium Core for safer entitlement handling.
