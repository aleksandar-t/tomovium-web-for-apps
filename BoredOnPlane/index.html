How it works

Prepare online. Enjoy offline.

BoredOnPlane is designed around the reality of travel: you may have internet before boarding, then nothing for hours. The app keeps built-in activities local, and generated trip packs are saved on-device so they are ready later.

  1. Pick or create a trip.Add the basic flight context the app needs.
  2. Prepare a pack before takeoff.Optional AI packs can include trivia, prompts, observation games, writing ideas, and cabin-friendly activities.
  3. Use it in airplane mode.Saved content stays available locally when the network is gone.
